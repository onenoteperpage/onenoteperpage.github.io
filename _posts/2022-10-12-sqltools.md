SQL Tools is installed to `C:\tools\sqltools\SQLTools.exe`

User profile can be located in either:

- C:\Users\`&lt;username&gt;`\AppData\Roaming\GNU\SQLToolsU
- C:\tools\sqltools\SharedDataU
